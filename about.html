Criminal Investigation and Forensic Science Seminar 2023

The conference will cover a wide range of topics related to criminal investigation, including crime scene analysis, evidence collection and preservation, forensic analysis of physical and digital evidence, interviewing and interrogation techniques, and the legal aspects of criminal investigation.

The conference is expected to be a comprehensive and informative event, with a wide range of topics related to criminal investigation being covered by expert speakers. These speakers will be presenting on various aspects of the investigative process, from the initial response to a crime scene to the presentation of evidence in court.
